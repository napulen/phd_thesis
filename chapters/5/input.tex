This section describes the process of transforming digital symbolic music scores into the input representations of the neural network. \refsubsec{symbolicscore} introduces examples symbolic music scores to be analyzed by the model. \refsubsec{frames} introduces the \emph{salami-slicing} of the score into \emph{frames}. \refsubsec{encodingpitchspelling} introduces the encoding of each \emph{salami slice} into a \emph{pitch-spelling} representation.
